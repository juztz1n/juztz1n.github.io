ABOUT ME

Hello there, my name's "Hazhir", and I currently live in Linköping, Sweden.

I've always really enjoyed complex puzzles and unique challenges, whether that'd be programming or solving real life problems. I work best under pressure, and in difficult environments because it's the challange that I enjoy.

Technology often seems like dark magic to most, and it seemed so to me as well. Realizing that there's an art to all of this dark magic, I decided at 11 to teach myself to become a magician!

Ever since then, I've dabbled in many programming languages, and many APIs. All the way from Python with Keras and TensorFlow to C++ with OpenGL, and Java with Minecraft.

I am a very fast learner and I can learn any programming language or API that you throw at me in a very short period of time. I am very dedicated to what I do and I'm always determined to create the best software to make people's lives better and easier.
